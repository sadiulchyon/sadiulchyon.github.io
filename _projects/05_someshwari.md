A companion study to my work on the Lubha River, this project traces the full impact chain of sand mining on the **Someshwari River** in Bangladesh from 1988 to 2025, working with **Jim Best**.

Using Google Earth Engine scripts built for seasonal sand-bar and water-dynamics change detection, the project reconstructs nearly four decades of channel and sediment response to extraction, connecting long-term morphological change back to specific phases of mining activity. The code and workflow are version-controlled and shareable so the analysis can be reproduced or extended to other rivers.

A manuscript on this work is in preparation for *Water Resources Research*.
